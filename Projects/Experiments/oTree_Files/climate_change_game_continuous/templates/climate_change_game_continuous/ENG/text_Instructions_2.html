Instructions II

After you and all group members made your decision, by some probability a disaster occurs that may destroy all or part of your earnings. How much you lose as a result of the disaster depends on how much you and your group members contributed to the collective pot. Every token invested into the collective pot reduces the impact of the disaster by {{d_impact_percentage}}%.



There is a {{dis_prob_percentage|floatformat:"0"}}% chance that a disaster occurs. In practice that means that at the end of this study, the computer spins a roulette wheel with ten slots: {{comp_dis_prob_number}} green slots and {{dis_prob_number}} red slots. The outcome of the roulette spin determines whether or not a disaster takes place.

  • If the roulette ends on one of the {{comp_dis_prob_number}} green slots, no disaster occurs.
  • If the roulette ends on one of the {{dis_prob_number}} green slots, a disaster occurs.
